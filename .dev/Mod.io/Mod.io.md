# Mod.io - Pinned Drops

## Mod profile
### Description
```html
<h4>Use Pinned Drops to see:</h4>
<ul>
<li>The exact amount of GP you collected during an hour of Thieving.</li>
<li>How many Slayer Coins you gained during a Slayer task.</li>
<li>The number of each type of gem dropped over a session of Mining.</li>
<li>How much Attack or Strength XP you gained in a dungeon.</li>
</ul>
<h4>Features:</h4>
<ul>
<li>Adds a Pin button to the top bar (next to the Potions menu button).</li>
<li>Hover over the Pin button to momentarily show the drops Panel, or click to pin the Panel in place.</li>
<li>Use the buttons at the top of the Panel to 'Pause' drop collection or 'Reset' and clear the list.</li>
<li>Sort the Panel by category, quantity, name, or order received (see Mod Settings).</li>
<li>Toggle whether to show the exact decimal values of XP gains or the usual rounded values (see Mod Settings).</li>
</ul>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
```
